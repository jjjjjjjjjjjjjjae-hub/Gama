Samsung Game Launcher Privacy Notice

Effective Date: August 24, 2021

At Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd. (“Samsung”), we know how important privacy is to our customers. Samsung is the data controller for Game Launcher. We created this Privacy Notice to make sure you understand how we collect and use your personal information.

Our Privacy Policy at https://account.samsung.com/membership/terms/privacypolicy also applies to your use of Game Launcher. Our Privacy Policy contains more information about how we use your data. It also includes information about your rights and how to contact us. Please read our Privacy Policy in addition to this Privacy Notice. However, this Privacy Notice shall always prevail over the Privacy Policy in relation to how we use your information for Game Launcher.

 

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Through Game Launcher, Samsung obtains and maintains information about you in various ways.

 

Information You Provide Directly

  • When you log in to your Samsung account, Samsung account information, such as your Samsung account ID, nickname, profile image, age , and country will be received by Game Launcher from your Samsung account; and
  • To provide responses when you contact Customer Service, we ask for information to verify your identity and the product you’re contacting us about. Your communications with Customer Service staff or others will be transmitted through our systems.

 

Information About Your Use of Game Launcher

In addition to the information you provide, we will collect information about your use of our Game Launcher through software on your devices and by other means. We will collect:

 

  • Information about your Game Launcher-enabled devices, unique device identifiers such as hashed IMEI, MCC (mobile country code), MNC (mobile network code), operating system versions and settings of Game Launcher and the devices you use to access Game Launcher;
  • Diagnostic, technical, error and usage information, including about how, when, and for how long you use Game Launcher and other information about your use of Game Launcher, such as how you interact with content offered through Game Launcher. We also collect information about games we recommend for you and the games you play on or through Game Launcher, such as app title installed and package name of the game you are playing, and games installed and deleted; and
  • Information from partners: When you play a particular game, we receive information about the game you have opened or begun playing (including the version of the game), how many times you open or begin playing the game and how long you play a particular game. In addition, we collect custom events in the games you play, such as when you complete a tutorial, scores and achievements, when you complete a level and when you fail a level.

 

We also may collect other information about you, your devices and apps, and your use of Game Launcher in ways that we describe to you at the time of collection or otherwise with your consent.

 

We use third-party analytics services, such as Firebase Analytics. The information we obtain may be disclosed to or collected directly by these providers and other relevant third parties who use the information, for example, to evaluate use of Game Launcher and to help administer Game Launcher. To learn more about Firebase Analytics, please visit https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/.

 

HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • to authenticate you so you may use Game Launcher;
  • to provide you with Game Launcher;
  • to improve and customize your experience with Game Launcher, such as delivering content and responses tailored to you and the way you interact with Game Launcher (for example, saving your personal achievements and progress) and providing content recommendations;
  • to link your Samsung account to connected Samsung or third-party devices, apps, features or services;
  • to respond to your requests, inquiries, and instructions made through Game Launcher;
  • to deliver advertising, including customized advertisements, sponsored content, and promotional communications (such as via push notifications, as well as through Samsung Ads: more details, including additional information we may obtain, how it is used and how to opt out of Samsung Ads, is available at https://policy.samsungrs.com/policy_ru.html);
  • to allow you to interact with and use participating third-party apps and services;
  • to operate, evaluate and improve Game Launcher and our business (including developing new products and services; enhancing and improving our products and services; managing our communications; analyzing our products, services and customer base; conducting market research; performing data analytics; and performing accounting, auditing and other internal functions);
  • to protect against, identify and prevent fraud and other criminal activity, claims and other liabilities; and
  • to comply with and enforce applicable legal requirements, relevant industry standards and our policies, including this Privacy Notice and the Privacy Policy.

 

Game Launcher may provide the ability to connect to or show other websites, apps, services, and other features, such as showing content you like or use from Samsung or third-party services. Third-party websites, apps, services, and features may operate independently from us and may have their own privacy notices or policies. Review them to ensure that you understand how they process your information. To the extent that any other linked or shown website, app, service, or other feature is not owned or controlled by us, we are not responsible for its content, use, or privacy practices.

 

ACCOUNT LINKING

Some third-party services may offer account linking, which lets you connect your Samsung account with the account of third-party services. Once account linked, you may be able to use some of the features from that third-party service without installation of that third-party's application. For example, if you log in to your Samsung account and finish the linking procedure in the applicable service, you may be able to use part of that third-party’s functions offered by a third-party.

Each time an account is linked, certain personal data will be sent to that third party, and that third party will send certain personal data to Samsung. Prior to account linking, we will inform you specifically as to what personal data will be shared, and for what purpose the specific personal data will be shared.

When you link accounts, we will also inform you on how to delink accounts.

HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION AND WHERE DOES IT GO?

How long your data will be retained depends on the legal basis relied upon to process your data. For example, data we process to perform our contract with you requires us to keep the data throughout the time you use Game Launcher. As long as you are an active user of Game Launcher, we will retain and process this data. Data such as your account information and device information and identifiers fall into this category.

 

Data we process to promote our business interests, such as your usage of Game Launcher, is only kept for as long as needed for the purposes for which it was collected. For example, this data may be collected to perform analytics so we can develop improvements to Game Launcher, or we may process data to keep a record of inquiries you made through or about Game Launcher to improve your customer service experience.

 

In addition, we may also process your data based on your consent. If you withdraw your consent, we will continue to process data collected prior to your withdrawal until the purposes for which such data was collected have been achieved.

 

Please note, although we aim to retain your data for the time period described above, your data may be processed longer pursuant to applicable law. For example, if a specific statute mandates we require a certain piece of data, we comply and retain that data until the required retention period expires.

 

As long as your data is retained by us your data will always be subject to appropriate safeguards.
